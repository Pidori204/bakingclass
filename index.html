Master the Art of Baking in One Day

What You'll Learn

Artisan Muffins

Master the art of creating perfectly domed muffins with various flavors: classic blueberry, double chocolate chip, and banana walnut.

Strawberry Tart

Discover the delicate balance of pastry making as you create a beautiful strawberry tart. Learn the secrets of making perfect sweet pastry dough, master the art of blind baking, and understand how temperature affects custard filling consistency. A perfect introduction to French patisserie techniques.

Classic Scones

Discover authentic British scone recipes. Create buttery, flaky scones with various mix-ins like cranberries, chocolate, and herbs.

Salt Bread

Learn professional techniques for creating artisanal breads, including our famous salt-crusted bread with a perfectly chewy interior.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Learn the science of cookie perfection: discover how different sugars affect chewiness, master the ideal butter temperature for perfect spreading, and understand the role of resting time in flavor development.

Croffle

Explore the art of lamination as you create this trendy hybrid pastry. Master the technique of folding butter into dough and learn how waffle iron temperature affects the final texture. A perfect blend of classic French pastry and Korean innovation.
